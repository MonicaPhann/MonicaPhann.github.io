Cooking is the art and science of preparing food by combining ingredients and applying heat or other techniques to transform raw elements into flavorful, nourishing meals. It encompasses various methods such as boiling, frying, roasting, baking, and steaming. Cooking is not just a necessity but also a cultural expression, bringing people together through shared meals and traditions.

About Cooking

I have always been interested in cooking ever since I got into high school. My high school had a culinary class, but unfortunately I wasn't able to take it. This led me to cooking on my own, trying out new recipies that I found on the internet and trying to create my own foods.

Cooking became a way for me to relieve some stress, whether it was from school, work, or other things. The good thing about cooking is that there is a tasty reward after putting in all your efforts. You also get to share the food to family to try, which always feels nice when they enjoy the food.

How to Cook

Cooking is a skill that can be learned through practice and experimentation. Here are some tips to help you get started:

  • Start with simple recipes by searching on google and gradually work your way up to more complex dishes.
  • Follow recipes carefully, but don't be afraid to make substitutions or add your own twist depending on what you have at home.
  • Invest in good quality cookware and utensils as it will make cooking easier in the long term and more enjoyable.
  • Experiment with different ingredients and flavors to make your own unique dishes and flavors.
  • Have a go-to dish, this helps you learn a dish really well to the point where you don't have to look at a recipe anymore!

Don't be afraid to cook something different or new. Everything is experimental and also does not have to follow a recipe directly!

Why You Should Cook

Cooking is a valuable skill that can benefit you in many ways:

  • Enjoyable Food: You can make foods that you would enjoy better and want to try out.
  • Save money: You can save a lot of money by cooking your own food.
  • Share with others: Cooking with friends or family is a great way to make memories as well as sharing lots of great food!
  • Relieve stress: In my opinion cooking can be very therapeutic and stress relieving as it takes your mind off of things easily.

Overall cooking is a great skill and hobby to pick up! It's useful because everyone needs to eat, so why not learn to make it yourself to learn the real values and efforts of cooking food!
